<h3>Ingredients:</h3>
<ul>
<li>1 Kg Potatoes</li>
<li>1 large Onion</li>
<li>500g Mince</li>
<li> Dash of Worcester Sauce</li>
<li>1 Beef Stock Cube</li>
<li>50ml Red Wine
<li>50 grams chedder cheese</li>	
</ul>

<h3>Method:</h3>

<p>Peel Potatoes, and cut into medium pieces, place in a pan, add salt and water and boil for 20 minutes or until potatoes soft.</p>
<p>Chop Onion finely and fry, then put to one side.</p>
<p>Put mince in a frying pan add onions and worcester sauce and stock cube as well as the wine.  Once the mince is darker put to one side. </p>
<p>50 grams chedder cheese</p>
